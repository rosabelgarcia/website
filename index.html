Artist Statement

As a choreographer, I am deeply driven by the powerful connection that exists between movement and music. This relationship is not just an artistic choice for me; it is a fundamental aspect of my creative identity. Drawing from my extensive dance background, I strive to create works that embody a contemporary fusion of diverse dance styles. This fusion reflects my belief in the inherent beauty of diversity and the importance of embracing our differences. Each piece I craft serves as a vibrant celebration of individuality, allowing me to explore and express the unique rhythms, emotions, and narratives that music evokes.

In my creative process, I begin by immersing myself in the chosen musical composition, contemplating the various dance styles that can be intertwined to enhance its expressive qualities. This exploration often leads me to discover unexpected connections between genres, whether it be the fluidity of contemporary dance, the structured elegance of ballet, or the raw energy of hip-hop. Once I have a clear vision, I establish movements through improvisation and manipulation of authentic steps that resonate with the music’s essence. This process is not only about creating visually appealing choreography; it’s also about digging deep into the emotional core of the piece and how that can be articulated through movement. A crucial aspect of my creative journey involves considering how both my audience and my dancers will experience the work. I aim to craft pieces that resonate on a personal level while also engaging and provoking thought within the audience. I believe that dance transcends mere performance; it serves as an invitation for connection—a bridge linking the dancers, the music, and the viewers. Each performance becomes an opportunity for shared experience, fostering dialogue between the art and those who witness it.

Ultimately, I strive to create choreography that not only showcases technical skill and artistic expression but also invites viewers to reflect on their own identities and experiences. I hope to inspire others to see the beauty in diversity and to embrace the myriad of ways we can express our stories through movement. In this way, my work is not just a reflection of my journey but a celebration of the rich tapestry of human experience.

- Rosabel Garcia
