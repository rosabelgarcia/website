Rosabel Garcia (She/Her) is a native of Cuba. She has always had a passion for dance and is constantly searching for ways to share her artistic talent with other individuals, both dancers and non-dancers. Rosabel moved to Miami, Florida at the age of 5, and trained in distinct styles such as ballet, modern, contemporary, jazz, hip-hop, and ballroom. She continued to train at Miami Arts Charter School with instructors Diana Ford, Theodore Talbot, Valerie Barreiro, and Hannah Baumgarten. She has attended numerous summer intensives including Joffrey Ballet Miami and Ruth Page International Dance Experience. She graduates from the University of South Florida in 2025 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Ballet and Dance Medicine and Science Certificate. During her years in college, she had the privilege to work with guest choreographers Kate Weare and Kelly King, as well as performing in faculty works. She also worked with companies Cuban Classical Ballet, Dance NOW! Miami, and Tampa City Ballet. Rosabel had the opportunity to participate in the USF Dance Paris Abroad Program in the Summer of 2024, presenting her to national artists.
